CODE OF CONDUCT

1. We focus on software and tech. This is not a place to discuss politics, especially identity politics
2. Treat others as you would like to be treated if you were in their place
3. If that is not possible, because you have different values, then at least let others have theirs in the same way that you claim yours
4. It is ok and natural to disaggree, but do not become rude or use name calling. That is considered immature.
5. If you feel offended, that is your choice. What other people say is their choice. There is no need to be offended by that.
   What they say is because of who they are, and that very often has nothing to do with who you are. So feeling offended is usually not smart.
6. If others are deliberately rude or obnoxious, usually ignoring them totally is the best thing to do.
   Those people are seeking for attention and derive their satisfaction from your response, especially the negative ones.
   Do not give them that pleasure and you will find they will get bored and walk away.
